Our Story

A Human Handshake in a Digital World

In an era of faceless AI, automated bots, and rising digital scams, we believe that true security starts with a human handshake and a foundation of absolute integrity.

Joshua began deep-diving into cryptocurrency in 2019, recognizing blockchain as the "Internet of Value." Having vacationed in Fort Myers for over 28 years before officially making it home in 2023, we felt a calling to use our knowledge to protect our local community.

We saw too many people left vulnerable to the pitfalls of this new frontier — and knew we had to help.

"Our work is rooted in our belief in God and the fundamental calling to love and treat our neighbors as ourselves. In the complex world of cryptocurrency, we believe that stewardship and honesty are paramount. We don't just see clients — we see our neighbors."

Guia Marisa and Joshua work as a team to ensure every neighbor feels empowered, not overwhelmed. Our mission is to replace the lack of trust in current systems with total confidence in your own digital sovereignty.

Our Approach

The Two Pillars of Protection

We believe you cannot secure what you do not fully understand. Every client goes through both pillars.

01
The Digital Security Audit

Before we build, we assess. We conduct a professional audit of your current digital footprint to identify vulnerabilities, hidden risks, and security gaps. Knowing exactly where you stand is the only way to move forward with confidence.

02
The Master Key System

Once the audit is complete, we implement your defense. Our signature Master Key system — a physical security binder — keeps your most vital information organized and offline. While your assets move at the speed of the internet, your protection remains as solid as a locked safe.
